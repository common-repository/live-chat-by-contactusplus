== Description ==

Chat with your website visitors live!

Increase your visitor conversion rate by engaging with your visitors while they are on your web-site.

Adds a *Live Chat* toolbar to the top right of your WordPress site.

Please note that by default, only the Contact Us option will be displayed in your website toolbar (this opens a contact form).
The Live Chat option is ONLY displayed when you are logged in to your ContactUsPlus.com account ready to service live chats.

Live Chat features include:

+ Service live chats from any browser, including smart phones
+ Service multiple visitors simultaneously
+ Canned responses (pre-configured text added to a chat with a single click)
+ Logs unsuccessful chat attempts
+ Design the toolbar to match your website
+ Works on mobile devices
+ Includes contact form as an alternative to live chat
+ Includes call-me form
+ Includes feedback tool
+ Reliable and secure
+ SPAM free
+ No need for your visitors to leave the page (or sale process) in order to send you a message
+ Complete chat history available for visitors and for the website administrator
+ The live chat service is provided by UserPulse.com. See [www.UserPulse.com](http://www.userpulse.com/) for a full list of features.

Free service includes 2 chats / day. See [www.userpulse.com](https://www.userpulse.com/securepages/create-account.jsp) for pricing.

Upon installation, a UserPulse.com account will be created for you to manage your settings and view all of your chats and messages. We'll email you confirmation details (to your WordPress.org email address), and forward completed contact forms and chat requests. We may also send you occasional news and updates. We will never share your details with anyone else (see the Privacy Policy and Terms of Use on our website).


== Installation ==

This section describes how to install the plugin and get it working.

1. Upload `contactusplus_live_chat.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the Live Chat by UserPulse plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
